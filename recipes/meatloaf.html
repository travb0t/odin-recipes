Why this recipe?

Get back to basics with this easy meatloaf recipe. It's quick, traditional, delicious, and it's sure to please everyone at your table.

Ingredients:

For the loaf:

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • One egg
  • One onion
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt & pepper

For the sauce:

  • Ketchup
  • Brown sugar
  • Mustard

Step by step:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
